Research

My research interests center on brain development, plasticity, and the interaction between sleep, cognition, and neuropsychiatric vulnerability. I am particularly interested in how early-life biological and environmental factors shape neural circuits and behavior across development, combining animal models, human data, and electrophysiological methods.
